All about me

By day, I work as a primary school teacher, a job that provides many challenges and has enabled me to develop great team-working and problem-solving skills along with no small amount of patience! Outside the classroom, I spend my time developing the skills and understanding necessary for a career in web development. My focus has been on building a confident knowledge of HTML, CSS and Javascript in order to develop websites and applications that are both interactive and responsive. Take a look at the links below to see what I've been up to and feel free to contact me if you have any questions about what I do. You might also want to check out my blog, let-there-be-code.com.
